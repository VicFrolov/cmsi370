FAQ

General

Our Purpose?

We want to connect the world, and allow communities to come closer together through our searches.

What can we use Tweet District for?

New to the neighborhood or want to know what's going on tonight? Just search for "party" or "bar" within a small radius of your current location, and you'll see which bars are hopping or what college parties are around you.
Are you an artists? See who's talking about you where, to help you decide on your next venue. Find local deals that are advertised on Twitter, and so much more!

What is this Longitude/Latitude business

For now, you must enter the latitude and longitude, but soon you will simply be able to type in an address, landmark or city for easier searches. Please use the link provided, and then manually put in the longitude/latitude. Sorry for the inconvenience!
